Suno 提示词：
A short escalating combo chime for a cozy merge game. Quick ascending scale of 3-4 bright bell notes going up in pitch, with a tiny sparkle burst at the end. More energetic and exciting than a single merge sound, but still soft and cute. Warm glockenspiel tones, satisfying momentum feeling. 1-2 seconds, no beat, no vocals, polished casual game combo SFX.

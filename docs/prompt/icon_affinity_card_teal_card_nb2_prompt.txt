Create one standalone friendship card icon for a cozy pastel flower-shop mobile game.

Use the provided reference style from the game's sofa and dress icons: bright saturated toy-like 3D object, rounded puffy volume, glossy soft highlights, thin warm edge definition, simple readable silhouette.

Icon concept:
- A stack of two or three friendship cards, clearly card-shaped.
- Dominant color must be bright teal / turquoise / mint-blue, not purple.
- Add coral-pink and cream accents, small flower or heart stamp on the top card.
- Make it obviously a card or card wallet, not a heart shape and not an envelope.
- Use rounded rectangle card corners, slight offset layers, visible card thickness.
- Keep it simple and chunky, like a toy prop icon.

Canvas:
- Square 1:1 image.
- Center the icon, generous margin.
- Pure solid magenta background (#FF00FF) for cutout.

Style rules:
- High saturation, strong volume, top-left glossy highlight, darker lower-right shading.
- Match the vivid sofa/dress button style.
- No UI frame, no badge background, no plaque, no white sticker border.
- No readable text, no letters, no numbers, no watermark.
- No purple dominant color.
- No realistic texture, no heavy black outline.

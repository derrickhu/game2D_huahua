== IMAGE 1: BOARD BACKGROUND (棋盘底图) ==

A seamless light-colored wooden tabletop surface, viewed from directly above (top-down flat view). This is the work table in a cozy flower shop where flowers are arranged. The wood grain runs horizontally with very subtle, fine texture.

STYLE:
- Warm birch/light oak wood tone, pale cream-beige color (#F5E6D0 to #EDD8C2)
- Very LOW saturation — the wood should look bleached/whitewashed, almost like a Scandinavian wood table
- Fine grain texture visible but NOT distracting — subtle enough that colorful flower items placed on top will pop
- Slight natural wood knot patterns (1-2 small ones) for organic feel
- Soft warm lighting from above, even illumination, no strong shadows
- Faint plank seam lines (2-3 vertical hairlines) to suggest wooden planks joined together
- Overall brightness: HIGH — this is a light, airy workspace
- Aspect ratio: roughly 7:9 proportion (width:height), portrait orientation
- NO TEXT, no labels, no objects on the table, no decorations — JUST the bare wood surface
- No vignetting, no dark edges — uniform color throughout

- The package should look inviting, like a present waiting to be opened
- Size: fills about 85% of a square cell, with magenta background visible around edges

- NO TEXT, no labels, no writing anywhere

A single piece of delicate white lace/tulle fabric draped flat, viewed from directly above (top-down view), on a solid bright magenta #FF00FF background. This represents a peek-through cover in a flower shop game — the player can glimpse what's underneath.

DESIGN:

- The lace should be about 30-40% opaque overall — enough to partially obscure but allow peeking
- Soft gentle wrinkles/drape texture, not perfectly flat
- Warm white tone, not cold/blue white
- Cute delicate style matching a cozy flower shop aesthetic
- Size: fills about 90% of a square cell
- ENTIRE background MUST be solid #FF00FF magenta — every pixel not part of the lace is magenta
- NO TEXT, no labels, no writing anywhere


== IMAGE 4: SHOP SCENE BACKGROUND (花店经营场景背景) ==

A wide panoramic interior view of a cozy flower shop, seen from the customer's perspective looking at the counter area. This serves as the background behind character portraits in a merge game.

DESIGN:
- WIDE landscape composition (aspect ratio approximately 3:1, very wide and short)
- Warm cream/ivory walls (#FFF8F0) with subtle plaster texture
- Left side: a charming arched window letting in soft golden sunlight, sheer curtain slightly drawn
- Center: the main counter/display area — a warm wooden counter surface visible at the bottom
- Right side: wooden shelving with a few small potted plants and flower vases (blurred/soft focus)
- Upper area: hanging dried flower bundles, a small vintage pendant lamp with warm glow
- A few small decorative elements on the wall: a tiny framed botanical print, a small wall-mounted flower basket
- Color palette: warm cream walls, honey wood tones, soft green plants, muted coral/pink flower accents
- Lighting: warm golden hour atmosphere, sunbeams creating soft light patches
- Depth of field: background elements are slightly soft/blurred (bokeh), keeping focus on the mid-ground where characters will be placed
- Overall feeling: warm, inviting, cozy, like stepping into a charming neighborhood flower shop
- The image should work as a BACKDROP — not too busy, not too plain, leaving space for character portraits in the foreground
- Style: soft painted illustration, gentle watercolor-like edges, warm color grading
- NO TEXT, no labels, no characters/people, no writing anywhere
- NO dark areas — keep everything bright and airy

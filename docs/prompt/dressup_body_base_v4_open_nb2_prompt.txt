Use the reference image only for the Huahua shop owner identity and soft 2D mobile-game art style. Redesign the body proportions and pose as specified below.

PROPORTION — young shopkeeper girl, NOT toddler:
- Cute stylized young girl / petite shop owner, about 3.0 to 3.4 head-heights tall.
- Still soft and cute, but less super-deformed than the previous tiny chibi.
- Head is large but not baby-like: about 32% to 38% of total standing height.
- Slightly longer neck and torso than the old chibi; arms and legs are short-cute but not baby tubes.
- Avoid toddler, child, baby, toy doll, 2-head chibi, fashion-realistic 5-head body.

POSE — critical for clothing layers:
- Same slight 3/4 view facing viewer's right.
- Standing naturally, feet planted, relaxed gentle smile.
- Arms hang naturally but are clearly away from the torso, leaving visible empty magenta gaps between both arms and the body.
- Elbows slightly bent outward, hands relaxed beside the hips, not touching the skirt/body.
- This gap is required so later sleeves/tops/dresses can be layered without being hidden by hands.

BASE BODY / NO OUTFIT:
- Completely bald smooth head, no hair, no hair clip.
- Open large brown eyes with sparkle highlight, gentle smile, rosy but subtle cheeks.
- Wear only a plain cream-white sleeveless camisole and plain cream-white fitted shorts.
- Bare feet, no shoes, no accessories, no patterns.

2D cute casual mobile game character, soft pastel, gentle flat-to-soft shading, warm brown soft line art, cozy flower shop vibe. Same general style as the reference, but with the updated less-childlike proportion.
